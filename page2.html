Choose your yarn ：

      
Yarn comes in many different weights: from super-fine baby-weight yarn all the way up to bulky-weight wool. Different patterns will recommend various types of yarns. But for beginners, I recommend using medium-weight (number four) acrylic, wool, or cotton yarn. You can buy this type of yarn online, or at any craft store! Medium yarn weight. I recommend that beginners choose a worsted-weight yarn. Smooth texture. It’s easier to see your stitches when you use a ball of simple, smooth wool or acrylic yarn. Light color. It can be hard to see exactly where to insert the hook when you use dark or multicolor yarn.
